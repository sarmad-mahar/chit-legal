1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how Chit ("the app", "we", "our", "developer") handles your information. By downloading or using Chit, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, you can discontinue use of the app.
Chit is developed and maintained by an independent developer based in Pakistan.

2. Information We Collect
2.1 Information you provide
When you first open Chit, you may optionally enter a display name and select a profile photo. This information is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.
2.2 Information you create
All notes, tasks, reminders, and settings you create within Chit are stored exclusively on your device using on-device storage (Hive local database). This content is never accessed, read, or transmitted by the developer.
2.3 Anonymous usage data (optional)
During onboarding, you may choose to optionally share your first name and app preferences anonymously with the developer to help improve the app. This is entirely opt-in. If you choose to share, the submission contains no device identifiers, no location data, and no personally identifiable information beyond what you voluntarily enter. You will not be contacted as a result of this submission.
2.4 Crash reports (anonymous technical data)
Chit uses Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC) to collect anonymous crash reports when the app encounters an error. These reports contain technical information only — such as device model, Android OS version, app version, and the location in the code where the crash occurred. No note content, task content, or personal information is included in crash reports.
2.5 Information we do NOT collect
We do not collect your email address, phone number, location, IP address, contact list, browsing history, or any other personal data. Chit does not contain advertising SDKs, third-party analytics, or tracking libraries of any kind.

3. How We Use Information
The limited anonymous data described above is used solely to:

Fix technical bugs and crashes
Understand general app preferences to guide future improvements

It is never used for advertising, profiling, or sold to any third party.

4. Data Storage and Security
All your personal data — notes, tasks, settings, profile name and photo — is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage directory. This directory is not accessible to other apps. The developer has no technical ability to access this data. If you uninstall Chit, all locally stored data is permanently deleted from your device.

5. Third-Party Services
Chit uses the following third-party service:
Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC)
Used for anonymous crash reporting, no personal user data is collected. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy. Crash reporting is enabled by default to help improve app stability and cannot be disabled in-app.
No other third-party services, SDKs, or APIs are used in this version of the app.

6. Permissions
Chit requests the following device permissions only when needed:
PermissionPurposePOST_NOTIFICATIONSTo deliver task reminders you setSCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARMFor precise reminder timing (Android 12+)READ_MEDIA_IMAGESTo let you pick a profile photo from your galleryWRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / MediaStoreTo save exported wallpapers to your gallery

7. Children's Privacy
Chit is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

8. Data Retention
Since all data is stored on your device, retention is entirely in your control. Notes and tasks moved to Trash are permanently deleted after 10 days automatically. You may delete all data instantly by uninstalling the app.

9. Your Rights
Since Chit stores no personal data on any server, there is nothing for us to provide, correct, or delete on our end. All your data is already in your possession on your own device. You have complete control over it at all times.
If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under GDPR. If you are located in California, you have rights under CCPA. In both cases, Chit's data-minimal design means those rights are already fully respected by default — we hold nothing about you.

10. Changes to This Policy
If this policy changes in a future app update, the updated policy will be made available at the same URL and the "Last updated" date at the top will be revised. Continued use of the app after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the new terms. Material changes will be noted in the app update release notes on the Play Store.
